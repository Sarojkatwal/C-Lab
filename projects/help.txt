The rules for this game are listed below.

1.Any player cannot choose the same particular position repeatedly.

2.If the sum of cards is exactly seventeen, then the particular player will win the game   instantly.

3.If the number of cards chosen is 5 but the sum does not cross 17, then the particular   player will win the game instantly.

4.Any players can wait and show the sum of cards at last if the sum of card’s value is less   than 17.whoever has the value nearer to 17 will win the game.

5.If the sum is greater than 18 the player will become out from the game.

6.Any player can know whether previous player are out or in waiting but does not know the     position of cards he has selected and sum of his card’s value.
